Grilled Pork Chops

Description

Easy to grill pork chops at home.

Ingredients

  • 4 ½ cups of pork chop brine
  • 4 bone-in pork ribs chops, each about 2 inches thick
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons of Morton's kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon of grounded cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon of sweet paprika
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil, plus more for the grill

Steps

  1. Brine the pork chops
  2. Heat the grill
  3. Season with the spice rub
  4. Grill the pork chops
  5. Allow the pork chops to rest
